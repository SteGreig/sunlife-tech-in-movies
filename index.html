We all have classic movies that we love.

We all love our modern technology.

But what happens when our modern technology gets in our classic movies?

Well, Let's Find Out…

Psycho

(1960)

Marion was on her way to starting a new life with her lover. All she needed was a place to wait out the storm.

If only she’d read the online reviews…

16 Candles

(1984)

On her 16th birthday, Sam’s life wasn’t going so well…

Social media could have reminded her family it was her big day and maybe things wouldn’t have started off so badly…

Wizard of Oz

(1939)

Dorothy and her new friends are off to see the Wizard!

But such a long and tiresome journey is even worse when you don’t know the way… a handy map app could have made all the difference.

Shawshank Redemption

(1994)

Andy Dufresne was facing a life in jail. But he had a place to go and now he’d found a way to get there…

CCTV would have kept a closer eye on things…

Casablanca

(1942)

An uncomfortable love triangle blossoms at Rick’s Café Americain…

But If Ugarte had sold the letters of transit online, Rick might have missed out on the beginning of a beautiful friendship…

Titanic

(1997)

A calm voyage from Southampton to NYC. There’s just one thing in the way…

If only they’d had radar on board…

You’ve got Mail

(1998)

Kathleen found her soulmate online… the owner of the bookstore chain running her out of business!

His dating profile could have been a spanner in the works…
